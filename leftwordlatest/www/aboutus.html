Welcome to Leftword

LeftWord Books (Naya Rasta Publishers Pvt. Ltd) is a New Delhi-based publishing house that seeks to reflect the views of the left in India and South Asia. We publish critical and analytical works on a range of subjects, and pay special attention to works on Marxist theory. We project the interests of the working people and movements for social transformation.
